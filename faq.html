Who is Grant Show?

Grant Show is an American actor born on February 27, 1962, in Detroit, Michigan. He gained widespread recognition playing Jake Hanson on the Fox drama series Melrose Place from 1992 to 1997, appearing in all 227 episodes. This role established him as a prominent television actor during the 1990s. He later achieved renewed success as Blake Carrington in The CW's Dynasty reboot, which ran from 2017 to 2022 for 108 episodes. Show studied acting at UCLA and has worked consistently in television for over three decades, demonstrating versatility across dramas, comedies, and various genres. His career longevity is notable in an industry where many actors struggle to maintain relevance across multiple decades and changing viewer preferences.
